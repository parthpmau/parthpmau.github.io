About Me

Hailing from Mumbai, little did I know that my journey to seek meaning in data would take me across oceans to the city of West Lafayette, Indiana. As a graduate student at the Krannert School of Management, Purdue University pursuing my Master of Science in Business Analytics and Information Management I am following my passion to make sense out of the 0’s and 1’s. The aforementioned journey started in Mu Sigma where I was introduced to the concept of data, and how every piece of data has a unique story to tell. A senior role at Ganit Inc helped me realize that I had just scratched the tip of the iceberg, and that there were higher mountains to scale. To sum it up, I am a decision science professional possessing an extensive analytical skillset, strong attention to detail and a significant ability to thrive in team environment who is looking to learn at every intermediate step of his journey.
